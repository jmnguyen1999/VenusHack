Systems Management

Systems managers' main duty is overseeing IT departments!
Their duties lie in managing an organization's networks and/or databases,
supporting employees through new systems, and often implementing security procedures.
Systems managers may work in both broad or close-knitted teams depending on department
and organization size! They often require knowledge of networks, information security
standards, operating systems, and hardware. People who are System managers also tend
to think very analytically, and business-focused in order to organize in lead in
a powerful way!
