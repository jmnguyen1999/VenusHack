Data Science

Those who work in data science like to solve problems through storytelling.
They follow business trends, objectively analyze real-world problems and their
solutions, and are open-minded when it comes to choosing an approach.
Data scientists are skilled in critical thinking and communication, and often
work with machine learning applications and mathematical algorithms. They also
work solo, until they need to present their findings to
company executives and key stakeholders.
