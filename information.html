Product Management

Those who work in product management are leaders with a passion
for tech products and their capabilities. They enjoy planning and organizing things,
with concern and empathy for those who they create products for.
Some of their skills include: understanding each part of
the tech stack to some degree, efficient task and time management,
and the ability to prioritize and pivot plans accordingly.
Product managers are leaders of their teams, and thrive in
fast-paced, quickly-changing environments.





Software Engineering

Those who work in software engineering love to find
creative solutions and learn new things. They may not be fond of
customer interactions, but they’re collaborative and have great interpersonal
skills within their team! Software engineers have deep technical knowledge
in data structures and algorithms, and can keep calm and positive attitudes
when encountering bugs in their code.Their work environment is
fast-paced and collaborative, but they’re also able to manage themselves.





Data Science

Those who work in data science like to solve problems through storytelling.
They follow business trends, objectively analyze real-world problems and their
solutions, and are open-minded when it comes to choosing an approach.
Data scientists are skilled in critical thinking and communication, and often
work with machine learning applications and mathematical algorithms. They also
work solo, until they need to present their findings to
company executives and key stakeholders.
