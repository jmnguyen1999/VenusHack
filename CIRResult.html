Computer Information Research

Computer information researchers are the people that study
and analyze new problems to find new and efficient ways technology
can be used! They are good at observing user needs, critically looking
at results, and testing existing systems! Computer information researchers
often are highly skilled in advanced mathematics, theory, core programming
structures and concepts, and statistical models. The characcteristics of
potential researchers are a great attention to detail and critical thinking!
