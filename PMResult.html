Product Management

Those who work in product management are leaders with a passion
for tech products and their capabilities. They enjoy planning and organizing things,
with concern and empathy for those who they create products for.
Some of their skills include: understanding each part of
the tech stack to some degree, efficient task and time management,
and the ability to prioritize and pivot plans accordingly.
Product managers are leaders of their teams, and thrive in
fast-paced, quickly-changing environments.
